Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes. Pediatric Tools is completely free to use. All 20+ clinical calculators are available without any subscription or paywall. There is an optional tip jar to support development.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All calculations are performed locally on your device. No internet connection is required.

What languages are supported?

Pediatric Tools is available in English, Portuguese (Brazilian), Spanish, and French. You can change the language in Settings without changing your device language.

Is my data shared?

No. The app does not collect, store, or share any data. Your preferences are stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I restore my tip jar purchase?

Yes. Tip jar purchases are managed by Apple and can be restored through the App Store. Open the app, go to Settings, then Tip Jar, and your previous purchases will be recognized automatically.

Is this app a substitute for clinical judgment?

No. Pediatric Tools is intended as a reference aid for healthcare professionals. All clinical decisions should be made based on professional judgment, clinical guidelines, and patient-specific factors.
