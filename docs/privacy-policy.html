Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Pediatric Tools ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App runs entirely on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require an account, does not use analytics services, and does not include any third-party tracking SDKs.

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (appearance, language, and portrait lock settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Tracking

The App does not track you. We do not use advertising identifiers, fingerprinting, or any other tracking technology.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional tip jar purchases. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services. All calculations are performed locally on your device without any network requests.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children. While the App is a pediatric medical tool, it is intended for use by healthcare professionals, not children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at rafaelvalim@gmail.com.
